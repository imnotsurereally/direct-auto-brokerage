Frequently asked questions

If you’re wondering it, someone else has too.

What exactly does an auto broker do?

An auto broker represents you, the buyer. We locate vehicles, negotiate pricing and terms, and coordinate the deal with the dealership so you avoid the pressure and confusion.

How do you get paid?

Typically through a flat broker fee and sometimes compensation from the dealer. We’ll always outline our fee structure up front so there are no surprises.

Do I still go to the dealership?

Yes, but only to sign and pick up your vehicle. All the hunting, negotiating, and structuring of your deal is handled beforehand.

Can you help if I’m not sure what car I want?

Absolutely. We’ll discuss your lifestyle, budget, and needs, then suggest vehicles and deal structures that make sense for you.
